**Agent level:** personal → team
**What:** "Loop engineering" is emerging as the named successor to ad-hoc prompting. **Evidence level:** Level 3 directional (3 independent practitioners, same month, same concept). Not yet full L3 (requires 10-20); this is leading-indicator territory.
**Key claims:**
- "Loop engineering" as named practice: prompting → loop architecture

- Osmani's six-component taxonomy: automations, worktrees, skills, connectors, subagents, external state
