*Written by Claude. Steered by Antti Tevanlinna. For Timo Laaksonen, CEO, F-Secure Corporation.*

We've had a hell of a year. A year ago we had AI tools on everyone's laptop and no idea what to do with them.


# What 12 Months of Action Looks Like

We started with 79 people using AI weekly. Within five months, over 400. Within two months of the training, virtually all our leadership teams had built their own agents. Our CRO became the transformation champion. We could not stop him from building.

40 business people trained directly. 15 internal trainers. They scaled it to 200+ on their own. Pretty awesome that we can do that.

We've turned fluffy aspiration into practical blockers we can solve. We have a movement with critical mass.

The board and CEO set the direction. But we did not formally strategise for how to get there. We just started building and let the strategy emerge from what people discovered.


# The Sequence Is Reversed

I could see the opportunity. We had steering groups. We had interesting discussions. But we weren't leading. We weren't moving fast enough.

What surprised me: our people could not envision the future until they built something with their own hands. The strategy didn't come from a planning session. It wrote itself — from action, from what people discovered when they actually started building.

Build first. Discover what matters. Then strategise.

It worked. That's all I needed to know.

Seven weeks. Your people build something real with their own work. Not a demo. One session per week — the rhythm lets it sink in. Leaders discover where the value is. Champions emerge and start telling others what's possible. You scale through your own people.

Next up we will pave the way to team and company agents. Momentum is ours to keep.
