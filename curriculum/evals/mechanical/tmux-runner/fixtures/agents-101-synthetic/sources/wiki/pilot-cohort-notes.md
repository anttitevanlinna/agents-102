## Early read (accounts #1–#4 retro)

- Mid-market accounts (#7, #8) **love it** — predictable floor, pay-for-growth
  above. This is the leadership thesis working.
- **The high-volatility SMB accounts (#3, #5) are the problem.** A spiky month
  produces a scary bill. #5 is the live fire drill this week.
- The floor matters enormously: too low and SMB sees variance; too high and we
  lose the "pay for what you use" story.
