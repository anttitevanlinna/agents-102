### Finding 8: Structural Insight — Vendor-Driven vs Practitioner-Driven Agentic Domains
**Source:** Synthesis across all practitioner discovery searches
**Date:** 2026-03-22
**What:** The reason HR/compliance/operations domains are dark is structurally different from what we assumed. It's not just that practitioners don't publish — it's that these domains are being **agentic-ified by vendors** (Workday Sana, SAP Joule, Thomson Reuters CoCounsel, RELEX), not by practitioners building custom workflows. In sales/GTM and customer service, practitioners built custom agent setups (LangChain, Claude, etc.) and published about them. In HR/compliance/operations, the agentic capability arrives embedded in vendor platforms. The practitioner is a buyer/deployer, not a builder. **This changes our research method:** for vendor-driven domains, evidence will come from trade press interviews and conference talks, not from blogs and X.com.
**Evidence level:** Level 3 (meta-pattern confirmed across 46 cycles of searching)
