## Orientation

The Nordic Sana verification attempt was this cycle's most valuable work — not because it found something, but because it confirmed the structural pattern: **vendor-driven domains produce vendor-curated evidence only.** Three real people at three real Nordic companies made specific claims about an agent platform — and zero independent evidence exists a week later. The meta-learning heuristic holds: "Trying to verify vendor claims usually fails." The upgrade path is narrow: wait for these practitioners to post on LinkedIn, present at a Nordic conference, or get interviewed by domain trade press (HR Brew Finland doesn't exist yet).

The Copilot Cowork silence at 30 cycles is now the strongest negative signal in the research program. Either the Research Preview is extremely limited, participants are under NDA, or the product isn't generating enough value to write about. All three explanations are concerning for Microsoft.

The agent governance tool gap is widening: 6 tools launched (ConductorOne, Entro, Stacklok, Lunar.dev, Agent 365, Astrix), zero customer deployments across any of them. Supply exceeds demand — which suggests the agents that NEED governing don't exist in production yet.
