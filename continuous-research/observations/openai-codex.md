# OpenAI Codex Team — AI-Native Product Team

**Type:** Born AI-native | **Size:** ~40 | **Evidence:** Moderate case
**Key sources:** Gergely Orosz / Pragmatic Engineer (2026), developers.openai.com guide

---

## How They Work

- ~40 people. 1 PM. Strong emphasis on empowerment and local decision-making.
- Codex writes **90% of its own app's code.**
- Team spans "low-level systems engineering, large-scale distributed infrastructure, product design, research, and user-facing experiences."
- Single engineers own features end-to-end, including people in leadership positions.
- "Ship on day one" culture — only works because everyone hired was already at this level.

## Key Insight

**Extreme autonomy at 40 people with 1 PM.** The ratio (1:40) is only possible because the team was assembled from people who don't need management — they need mission, tools, and each other.
