Privacy

This site uses Umami for analytics. Umami is privacy-focused by design:

  • No cookies. Nothing is stored on your device.
  • No personal data. No IP addresses, no fingerprinting, no identifiers.
  • No cross-site tracking. Each site is isolated.

The quizzes (Strategic Profile Check, Readiness Assessment) track anonymous answer patterns to understand how organizations think about AI agents. No answers are linked to any individual.

We do not use any other tracking, advertising, or third-party analytics services.
