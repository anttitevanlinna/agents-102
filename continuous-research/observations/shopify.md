# Shopify — Mandate-Driven AI Transition

**Type:** Mandated transition | **Size:** ~10,000 | **Evidence:** Moderate case
**Key sources:** TechCrunch (Apr 2025), Fortune (Apr 2025), Foster Fletcher critique, MetricSherpa analysis

---

## The Mandate

Tobi Lutke's memo (April 2025): "AI use is no longer optional."

**Specific structural changes:**
- Teams cannot request headcount before demonstrating AI cannot do the job
- AI competency included in performance reviews and hiring
- Applied to Lutke himself first — he uses the tools

**Results:**
- Scout tool built by small non-R&D team
- 50+ features shipped in 2025
- Changed decision structures, not just tool access

## Why It Worked (Conditions)

1. **Leader personally practices** — Lutke applied mandate to himself first. Credibility.
2. **Changed decision structures** — "prove AI can't do it before hiring" changes incentives at every level
3. **Enabling conditions alongside** — tools provided, not just expectations set
4. **Small, non-R&D team built Scout** — demonstrated that non-traditional builders could produce

## The Backlash

Documented criticism:
- **"Performative productivity"** — prompting ChatGPT becomes a checkbox (Foster Fletcher)
- **"Erodes psychological safety"** — AI competency in performance reviews creates anxiety
- **"Discourages genuine experimentation"** — people play safe to demonstrate usage metrics
- **MetricSherpa analysis:** Mandate produced real results AND real resistance simultaneously

## The Lesson

Mandates work when:
- Leader mandating is personally practicing
- Mandate changes decision structures (not just expectations)
- Enabling conditions exist alongside
- Peer structures complement the mandate

Mandates backfire when:
- Leader mandating doesn't use the tools (70% of C-suite — HBR)
- It's pure cost-cutting (Duolingo, Fiverr)
- Without enablement ("you must" without "here's how")
- Punishes non-adoption rather than rewarding creative adoption

## Key Insight

**The strongest approach may be Shopify's structural change (prove AI can't do it) combined with bottom-up enablement (training, peer networks, WOW moments), NOT the mandate culture (performance reviews, compliance metrics).** Separate the structural redesign from the compliance pressure.
