## Orientation

Three signals this cycle:

1. **Claude Cowork feature velocity is the story Microsoft should worry about.** Persistent threads, scheduled tasks, M365 MCP connector, 11 business-domain plugins — all in one month. While Copilot Cowork has 26 consecutive zeros for independent reviews, Claude Cowork is shipping features that directly address the multi-turn memory gap and business-user accessibility. The M365 MCP connector means Anthropic is reaching INTO Microsoft's ecosystem, not waiting for permission.

2. **Legal domain approaching Level 3 convergence.** Harvey AI ($195M ARR, 500+ customers, 42% Am Law 100) + CMS (7,000+ lawyers, 118hrs/yr saved) + A&O Shearman (4,000 staff, 43 jurisdictions) + Crosby/Daniels (cycle 56) = fourth domain approaching convergence after coding, CS, and finance/accounting. The Level 4 meta-pattern (rules-based + verifiable + talent-constrained domains converge first) strengthens — legal work is codified, output is verifiable against statutes/precedent, and legal talent is expensive.

3. **Platform proliferation outpacing deployment.** Zoom, Alibaba Wukong, Hexaware Agentverse, Agentforce Contact Center, Snowflake SnowWork (last cycle) — 5+ new platform announcements in 2 weeks, zero new horizontal platform deployment evidence. The gap between announcement velocity and deployment reality is widening, not closing.
