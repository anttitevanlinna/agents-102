The Gap

The gap is not between strategy and execution.

Everyone has an AI strategy now. The board has seen the McKinsey report. A governance framework has been approved. An initiative portfolio is underway. Eighteen months from now, nobody in leadership will have seen an agent work with your company's data. The frameworks will govern an abstraction. The initiatives will produce slides. And the transformation will feel like theatre -- because it is. Previous transformations did not require this. You could envision a digital company without building software. Agents are different. You cannot lead what you cannot picture. And you cannot picture it without building it. The gap is between strategy and competence.

Without agent competence

  • Governance frameworks that govern an abstraction
  • Initiative portfolios that produce slides
  • Transformation that feels like theatre
  • Leadership that cannot picture what's possible

With agent competence

  • Your people have built real agents with your data
  • Leadership has seen what's possible firsthand
  • Strategy grounded in lived experience
