# Nordveil Pricing Strategy 2026 (Confluence — Pricing space)

_Owner: Ingrid Solberg · Last edited: 2 Jun 2026 · Status: DRAFT for board_

## The problem with per-seat

We charge per dispatcher seat. Dispatchers share logins to avoid buying seats:
a 12-dispatcher operation runs on 4–5 paid seats. Seat count has been flat for
three quarters while **dispatched routes per account grew ~40% YoY**. We are
giving away the growth.

## The proposed model

Move to **usage-based pricing on dispatched routes**, with a committed monthly
minimum (a floor) plus per-route overage above the floor. Keep a small per-seat
component for admin users only.

Leadership thesis (CEO, CFO): usage-based aligns price with value, unlocks the
expansion we're currently leaving on the table, and lifts net revenue retention.

## Risks we know about

- **SMB bill shock.** Smaller operators have spiky route volumes. A bad month
  could produce a bill they didn't expect. This is the risk the pilot exists to
  measure. (See pilot cohort notes — the cohort is SMB-heavy on purpose.)
- Sales org doesn't yet know how to sell or explain it.
- Finance needs the floor set right or we trade predictable revenue for variance.

## Decision

Go/no-go at end of Q3, gated on pilot data. Ingrid owns the pilot and the
recommendation.
