## Notes

### Key Practitioner Signals
- **Klarna reversal is the dominant narrative.** 10 distinct articles covering the arc from "700 FTE replaced" to "rehiring humans." CEO Siemiatkowski's admission that cost-over-quality drove the decision is widely quoted. Gergely Orosz (Pragmatic Engineer) has a critical analysis questioning the initial claims.
- **Sierra $100M ARR in 21 months** is the strongest platform-level signal. Voice interactions overtook text by Sept 2025. Customer list spans both tech (Discord, Rivian, Ramp) and traditional (ADT, Bissell, SiriusXM). Sacra estimates $150M ARR by Jan 2026.
- **SeatGeek phased deployment** is the best "how to do it right" case: start with one high-volume topic (authenticated users, "where are my tickets?"), prove value, expand. Satisfaction doubled from 34% to 70%.

### Deployment Evidence (Strongest)
1. **Klarna** — 2.3M convos/month, reversal to hybrid (Sweden/Nordic)
2. **Sierra** — $100M ARR, hundreds of enterprise customers, voice > text
3. **Salesforce Agentforce** — 12K+ customers, $100M+ cost savings, 330% ARR growth
4. **Intercom Fin** — 66% avg resolution, 6K+ customers, resolution rate climbing 1%/month
5. **SeatGeek/Zendesk** — 51.5% auto-resolution, 57K queries, satisfaction doubled
6. **Gjensidige** — Eva/Sofie/Frank multi-agent system for claims (Nordic)
7. **PhonePe** — 80% query automation serving 300M+ users (via Freddy)
8. **Duolingo** — 80%+ deflection (via Decagon)

### Counter-Evidence (Critical)
- **Gartner Feb 2026 prediction:** 50% of companies that cut CS staff due to AI will rehire by 2027
- **Forrester Oct 2025:** Half of AI-laid-off workers will be quietly rehired at lower pay
- **55% of companies regret AI redundancies** (TechFinitive compilation)
- **Quality is the production killer:** LangChain survey shows 32% cite quality as top barrier
- **52% of customers prefer human agents** for empathetic support; 88.8% want human option available

### Nordic Findings
- **Gjensidige (Norway)** — Most genuinely agentic Nordic deployment; 3 named agents (Eva, Sofie, Frank). NHH academic coverage provides independent evidence.
- **Nordea (pan-Nordic)** — 12 chatbots, 220K+ convos/month, but chatbot-level only. No evidence of autonomous actions on accounts.
- **Telia (pan-Nordic)** — 30% chat automation via Ultimate.ai/Zendesk. Chatbot-level. Telia Finland article explicitly states "people are at the core."
- **Telenor (Norway)** — Telmi virtual agent since 2019 via boost.ai. Can retrieve account info but primarily conversational routing.
- **NO new Nordic agentic deployments found** beyond what was already in archive. The gap between Gjensidige (truly agentic) and Nordic banks/telecoms (chatbot) remains stark.
