Cell division model for AI-native deployment: seed one AI-native-majority team with PM in generation loop. When people "get it," they don't return — they seed new AI-native teams. Each team spawns new teams. Exponential, not linear. Coaching stays available to keep new cells viable.

The constraint is AI-native majority per team. Drop below majority and old patterns reassert (amplification paradox). The seed people are the scarce resource — not tools, not training, not budget.

Math: 1 seed → 3 teams (Q1) → 9 (Q2) → 27 (Q3). Covers a 500-person org in 9 months.

Not a mandate (Amazon). Not self-organizing (Every). Not champion-network (Citi). Not rotation-and-return. A fifth model: **cell division with maintained majority.**

**Gap:** Zero examples in the research. Novel deployment model. Needs: first company to run it and report results.
