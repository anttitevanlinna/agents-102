### Finding 3: Claude Managed Agents — first independent practitioner reports (Level 2)

**Date:** April 2026
**Agent level:** Company
**What:** Two independent practitioners published honest assessments within two weeks of April 8 launch. Zalta (LLMOps engineer): net positive — per-tool permission scoping "makes Managed Agents more production-ready than LangGraph, CrewAI, or AutoGen" which lack it; deployed in 10 minutes, 3 API calls; "a few cents" for 10-min session. unicodeveloper: critical — four structural concerns: (1) Claude-only, vendor lock-in, no migration path; (2) multi-agent coordination + self-evaluation still research preview, not in beta; (3) every tool call runs through Anthropic infrastructure (data privacy concern for sensitive workloads); (4) fleet scaling costs accumulate. **Evidence level:** Level 2 — two independent practitioners, different conclusions, honest disagreement. The split itself is signal: not a slam dunk, real tradeoffs.
