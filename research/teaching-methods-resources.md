## Teaching Insights from Resources

Based on the comprehensive research, here are patterns in how LLM agents are being taught:

1. **Progressive Complexity:** Most courses start with agent fundamentals, then progress to multi-agent systems, production patterns, and advanced techniques.

2. **Framework Focus:** Different instructors emphasize different frameworks (LangGraph, CrewAI, Phidata, Mastra), but core concepts are similar.

3. **Practical Projects:** Strong emphasis on building real projects rather than theory-only approaches.

4. **Tool Integration:** Heavy focus on how agents use tools and APIs effectively.

5. **Evaluation & Testing:** Growing emphasis on how to evaluate and test agents in production.

6. **Prompt Engineering:** Recognized as critical skill across all resources.

7. **Multi-Agent Patterns:** Increasingly popular topic for advanced learners.

8. **RAG Integration:** RAG-agent systems are positioned as a key pattern for knowledge-intensive applications.

9. **Visual Explanations:** Trend toward visual representations of agent concepts and architectures.

10. **Free Resources:** Many high-quality free resources available (UC Berkeley MOOC, HuggingFace course, DeepLearning.AI, etc.) alongside premium courses.
