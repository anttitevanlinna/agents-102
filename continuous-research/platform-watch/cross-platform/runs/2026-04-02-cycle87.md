## Orientation

**The dual IPO race is the new meta-narrative.** Both OpenAI ($852B, targeting Q4 2026/Q1 2027) and Anthropic ($380B, targeting Q4 2026) are racing to public markets. This will drive product decisions for the next 6-9 months — expect both companies to optimize for metrics that look good in an S-1, not necessarily for enterprise agent capabilities.

**The credit cliff finding is sharper than expected.** We hypothesized silence = low adoption. Now we have the perfect control group: same credit system, same transition date, different user populations. Developers complain loudly. Business users don't make a sound. The credit cliff became an accidental adoption experiment.

**Agentforce is the only vertical SaaS platform showing real momentum beyond its initial domain.** $800M ARR + 180 ITSM organizations = first quantified evidence of domain expansion. Still vendor-sourced for ITSM, but the revenue growth is independently verifiable.

**Agent security is no longer theoretical.** Meta Sev 1 + OpenClaw CVSS 9.9 + Okta building dedicated product = the confused deputy problem is production-real. This is now Level 3 convergence.
