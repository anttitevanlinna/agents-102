### Key Signals
1. **AI SDR market is vendor-dominated.** 33 of 80 links classified as vendor-claim. The AI SDR space is heavily commercialized — nearly every comparison, benchmark, and "results" article is published by an AI SDR vendor (SuperAGI, AiSDR, Landbase, Jeeva, 11x). Independent deployment data is scarce.

2. **The 85% AI SDR failure rate was NOT found in any primary source.** Query 3 specifically searched for this claim. The closest finding was Salesforce's own article citing an MIT study that "95% of AI pilots fail to generate ROI" — a broader stat about all AI, not SDR-specific. The 85% figure from the archive file was already tagged [SOURCE NEEDED] and remains unverified.

3. **Salesforce Agentforce SDR has real implementation content.** Multiple practitioner-analysis articles (#26, #27, #28, #30) provide genuine technical walkthroughs and failure pattern documentation — useful for understanding what actually goes wrong vs. vendor success stories.

4. **HubSpot Breeze results are thin.** Query 4 returned mostly HubSpot product pages. One customer mention (Sandler: 25% more engagement, 4x leads) from search snippet. No independent third-party measurement of Breeze results found.

5. **Qualified/Piper is the most documented AI SDR.** 500+ deployments claimed, named customers (Greenhouse $27M pipeline, Asana 22% pipeline increase, Litmus 2x meetings). However, all evidence flows through Qualified's own channels — no independent verification found in this search.

6. **Aprimo search was a vendor echo chamber.** 8 of 10 results were Aprimo's own blog/product pages. The Gartner Leader recognition (external) is the only independent signal.

7. **Nordic-specific results are extremely thin.** Query 7 returned zero Nordic AI sales agent deployments. Only ecosystem-level data: 15 Nordic AI-in-sales startups (Tracxn), 510 Nordic AI startups total. No named Nordic company deploying AI sales agents found in this search (VTT and Klarna were found in prior research, not in these searches).

The hybrid model signal (2.8x more pipeline with AI+human vs. AI-only) is the most useful data point but its source (Pavilion study) needs primary verification.
