Practitioner observes: MobAI/CollabAI has been around for 1+ years and has not crossed the chasm. Not even close.

This is confirmed by cycle 75 research: one hard metric (PALO IT, a consultancy), zero business results, zero convergence, a small Nordic network tracing back to one person (Joe Justice). Compare to compound engineering which reached Level 3 convergence in similar timeframe.

**Possible explanations identified in discussion:**
1. Requires facilitation — doesn't scale without a trained person in the room
2. No measurable outcome — produces learning, not artifacts. Hard to justify ROI
3. Solo + AI may simply be more productive for output
4. MobAI may be a stepping stone people move past, not a destination — valid on-ramp but practitioners outgrow it quickly

**Assessment:** MobAI is a valid Phase 1 on-ramp (the WOW moment, group learning) but the methodology itself has not produced business results. The value may be entirely in what it leads TO (individual practice → SwarmAI → scaled AI architecture), not in what it IS.
