# AMPECO — Small Company Transitioning to AI-Native Engineering

**Type:** Transitioning | **Size:** Small | **Evidence:** Signal
**Key sources:** agents-102 continuous research cycles

---

## Observed Practices

- Cancelled iteration demos (shipping daily makes them obsolete)
- Moved from weekly sprints to continuous daily releases
- 20,000+ automated tests as quality gate
- The ritual elimination pattern: when shipping is continuous, coordination ceremonies designed for batch delivery become unnecessary

## Key Insight

**Rituals that required coordination become obsolete when shipping is continuous.** Daily delivery eliminates the need for biweekly sprint ceremonies, demo meetings, and release planning. The process simplifies itself.
