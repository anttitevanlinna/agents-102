The quotation marks suggest a direct quote but the exact phrasing may be paraphrased. Not attributed to a specific interview or date. **Flag: quote attributed to CEO but not precisely sourced. Verify against original source.**
- **Verbatim passages:** No passages appear to be copied verbatim from external sources. The writing style is consistent throughout and clearly original analytical prose.
- **Metrics/statistics:** Numerous specific metrics are used (e.g., "400,000-600,000 interactions/year", "50,000 hours/year", "44% reduction", "118 hours per lawyer per year", "55% resolution", "54% recruiter capacity", etc.). Most are attributed to sources but many come from vendor case studies. Attribution is present but not always with direct links. **Low risk -- factual data points with attribution.**
- **Overall copyright assessment:** LOW RISK. Content is primarily original analysis and synthesis. Metrics are factual data points. No substantial reproduction of source material.

1. **sales-marketing.md: "85% of fully autonomous AI SDR deployments shut down within 6 months"** -- This is the anchor claim of the entire file, used in both the orientation summary and the Sendoso/Gem-E finding. No primary source is cited. This needs a source or must be reframed as anecdotal/estimated.

2. **compliance-legal.md: Three unsourced market statistics in orientation summary** -- "23% to 54% adoption" (ACC/Everlaw named but not linked), "$1.5B to $3B+ market growth" (no source), "40% of enterprise applications by end of 2026" (no source). These are presented as authoritative facts.

3. **hr-people-ops.md: IKEA "$1.4B additional revenue" from reskilling 8,500 employees** -- A very specific and remarkable claim with no source link. Needs verification or removal.

4. **hr-people-ops.md: Siemens finding sourced from aggregator site** -- The Cubeo.ai listicle is not a primary source. Metrics (55% resolution, 40% ticket reduction, 30% satisfaction uplift) cannot be independently verified from this source.

5. **sales-marketing.md: VTT Finland sourced from generic Salesforce product page** -- The URL does not lead to a VTT-specific case study. If Salesforce removes VTT from the page, this finding becomes unverifiable.

6. **product-strategy.md / sales-marketing.md: Crayon "22% increase in competitive win rates"** -- Stated in two files with no named client. Vendor-sourced claim used as evidence without qualification.
