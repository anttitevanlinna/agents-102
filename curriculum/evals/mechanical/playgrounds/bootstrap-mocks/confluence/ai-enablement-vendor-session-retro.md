# AI Enablement Vendor Session — Retro (May 2026)

Vendor (name redacted) ran a 90-minute "agentic enablement" session for the platform leads. Goal was to give skeptics a concrete picture. Result: not what we wanted.

**What happened.** Vendor demoed five canned scenarios, all clean-path. Q&A drifted to abstractions. Paavo asked three concrete change-review questions; vendor punted to "depends on your context." Sari and Jukka were polite; Anneli watchful; Paavo mostly silent after Q3.

**What we learned.**
- Demos do not convert principled skeptics. Paavo's bar is *"safe Terraform change at 3am"* — a polished demo at 2pm registers as the wrong shape of evidence.
- Vendor framing of "agentic transformation" is too far from the room's actual change-review and on-call work to be useful. Stay in our domain.
- The room is not pro/anti-AI in a binary; it's three groups (builders / waiters / principled skeptics) with very different unblockers.

**Decisions out of the retro.**
1. No more vendor sessions for the next quarter.
2. Internal "walk through a real agentic move" sessions — sub-team leads run their own.
3. The Q3 offsite is the venue where shipped-things and written-bars get compared. Vendor sessions don't substitute.

**Status:** kept as a reference point (counter-example), not as a stale claim. Useful when arguing against another vendor pitch.
