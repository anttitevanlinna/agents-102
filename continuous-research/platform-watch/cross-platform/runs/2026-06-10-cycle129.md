**What:** The "counter-evidence" to Pattern 48 actually confirms it at a deeper level. Named positive-ROI deployments are ALL scoped, single-purpose agents with defined baselines:
- JPMorgan COiN: 360,000 lawyer-hours/year (continuous since 2017)
- Klarna: $60M saved (Q3 2025), equivalent to 853 FTE agents
- General Mills supply chain: $20M+ savings, 5,000+ daily shipments autonomously assessed
