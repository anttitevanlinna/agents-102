## What to Watch For

When adding or updating a company observation:

1. **How did the AI-native team FORM?** (Greenfield? Transform-in-place? Mandate? Seed practitioner?)
2. **What's the team size and composition?** (Roles, specialists vs generalists)
3. **What do they actually DO differently?** (Not tools — working patterns)
4. **What verification/quality approach?** (How do they know output is good?)
5. **What broke or didn't work?** (Failures are more instructive than successes)
6. **What's the leadership model?** (Player-coach? Flat? Traditional?)
7. **Is this sustainable?** (Early results or proven over time?)
