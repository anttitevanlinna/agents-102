## Orientation

The cross-platform picture sharpens: **the announcement-to-deployment gap is the defining feature of Q1 2026.** Every platform has shipped major announcements (Foundry GA, Workspace Studio GA, Frontier, Copilot Cowork, E7 bundle) and none has independent deployment evidence. The most credible independent voices (Mollick, SAMexpert, Salesforce Ben) are skeptical. OpenAI's defensive consolidation (super-app merge) driven by Anthropic's enterprise momentum is the biggest strategic shift this cycle — it signals that even the market leader sees product fragmentation as a vulnerability. The Agentforce expansion beyond customer service is worth tracking but remains vendor-sourced. The credit cliff (Google, March 31) and E7 GA (Microsoft, May 1) are the next two moments where real demand gets tested.
