### Finding 1: Cognitive Debt / Comprehension Debt Confirmed at Level 3 (Five Independent Sources)

**Source A:** https://simonwillison.net/2026/Feb/15/cognitive-debt/ — [practitioner direct], Feb 15, 2026
**Source B:** https://addyosmani.com/blog/comprehension-debt/ — [practitioner direct], Mar 14, 2026

**Source D:** https://www.geoffreylitt.com/2026/07/02/understanding-is-the-new-bottleneck.html — [practitioner direct], Jul 2, 2026
**Source E:** https://www.revenuecat.com/blog/company/inside-product-engineering/ — [practitioner direct], Jun 27, 2026
**Date:** Feb–Jul 2026 (convergence window)
**Agent level:** team
**What:** Five independent practitioners across different organizations and time windows converge on the same AI-native team failure mode: understanding drifts from AI-built code faster than humans can track, disabling meaningful participation. Willison (Feb): coined "cognitive debt" from Margaret-Anne Storey's definition. Osmani (Mar): independently named "comprehension debt" with quantitative grounding — N=78 study showing 77% failure on maintenance tasks without AI; Anthropic RCT showing 17% comprehension gap (50% vs 67%); AI generates 140–200 lines/min vs human comprehension 20–40 lines/min (5–7x speed gap). Shopify/Thawar (Apr): named "brain atrophy" risk at VP Engineering level, implemented weekly demos (not velocity metrics) as comprehension test, confirmed AI reversion rate at baseline parity. Litt (Jul 2, Notion, AI Engineer conference): "Understanding is the new bottleneck" — proposes code explainer docs, embedded comprehension quizzes, micro-worlds as structural solutions. RevenueCat (Jun): merged PM into engineering, renamed to "product engineers" who actively write code — organizational restructuring explicitly to prevent cognitive debt accumulation. Willison amplified Litt on same day.
**Evidence level:** Level 3 — five independent sources across 5 months, different organizations, different angles, no coordination between sources. Osmani's N=78 provides quantitative grounding. This is the strongest L3 convergence the AI-native teams track has produced.
**Key claims:**
- 5–7x speed gap between AI generation (140–200 lines/min) and human comprehension (20–40 lines/min) — Osmani, N=78 study
- 77% of unrestricted AI users failed 30-minute maintenance task without AI (vs 39% in scaffolded group) — Osmani, N=78
- 17% comprehension gap in Anthropic RCT (AI-assisted scored 50% vs 67% control) — Osmani
- Shopify AI reversion rate at baseline parity — first production quality-parity validation
- RevenueCat PM→product engineer role change: org-level restructuring response to prevent cognitive debt
