### Finding 2: Level 4 Meta-Pattern Candidate — Why Accounting Converges First

**Source:** Cross-domain analysis of convergence patterns across 55 cycles
**Date:** March 2026
**Agent level:** Company
**What:** Three structural factors explain why finance/accounting hit Level 3 before other non-coding domains: (1) **Rules-based with verifiable correctness** — GAAP/tax code provides ground truth, accuracy is measurable. (2) **Acute talent shortage** — 75% of US CPAs near retirement, CPA exam registrations at historic lows, creating genuine pull. (3) **High-volume structured data** — invoices, transactions, reconciliations are structured and digital. **Meta-pattern:** Autonomous agents converge first in domains where rules are codified, correctness is verifiable, and talent supply is constrained. Test against legal (Garfield AI signal) and compliance (Noru signal) from previous cycles.
**Evidence level:** Level 4 candidate (cross-domain structural pattern)
