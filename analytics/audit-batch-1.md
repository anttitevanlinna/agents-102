The quote is short (8 words total across two fragments) and used for factual reporting/commentary -- **low risk** under fair use / journalistic commentary norms, but it is a direct quote.
- **No verbatim passages detected.** The writing style across all findings is consistent analytical summary, not copy-pasted from source material.
- **Metrics/statistics are properly attributed** to specific sources throughout (vendor case studies, press releases).
- **Overall assessment: LOW copyright risk.** Content is primarily factual summary and original analysis. The three-tier framework (Orientation Summary), the Klarna arc narrative, and the "Key Patterns" section are all original analytical synthesis. The finding descriptions are factual summaries, not reproductions of source articles.

### finance-accounting.md

- **Potential quote (line 104):** Klarna framing described as "replace half the staff" -- this appears to be the author's paraphrase, not a direct quote. **No issue.**
- **No verbatim passages detected.** The orientation summary and all finding descriptions read as original analytical synthesis. The three-wave adoption pattern framework is original analysis.

The Market Context Data Points section (lines 213-220) has 8 statistics with weak or missing attribution (see traceability section above). - **Overall assessment: LOW-TO-MODERATE copyright risk.** The finding descriptions are original analytical work. The Market Context section carries moderate risk due to unattributed statistics that may come from proprietary reports.
