# `continuous-research/` — Open for Contribution

This directory is the **public knowledge base** of the Agents 102 project. Unlike the rest of this repository — which is proprietary to Bosser Oy (see root [`COPYRIGHT.md`](../COPYRIGHT.md)) — the research system is intentionally open for use and contribution by the broader community of agentic industry leaders.

## What You Can Do

- **Read and quote** any finding, synthesis, pattern, or observation here — with attribution.
- **Fork and redistribute** the research corpus, in whole or in part, provided the evidence standards documented in the root `CLAUDE.md` are respected and third-party sources cited inside remain credited to their original authors.
- **Contribute findings, user signals, and observations** back into the research base. Research runs continuously; practitioner contributions sharpen it.

## Contribution Terms

By contributing into `continuous-research/` — via pull request, direct push (for trusted research agents and maintainers), or any other means — you grant **Bosser Oy and the community** a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, adapt, redistribute, and build upon your contribution as part of the public research base. You retain authorship attribution where applicable.

Contributions must honour the research guidelines in the root `CLAUDE.md` — the evidence ladder, source-type classification, freshness rule, obfuscation rule for user signals, and the three admission gates per finding. Contributions that ignore those rules will be declined or edited to fit.

## Third-Party Content

Links, quotations, and excerpts from third-party sources inside these research files remain the property of their original authors and are used here under fair-use / citation norms. Nothing in this open-contribution notice modifies those third-party rights.

## What Is NOT Covered Here

The curriculum, content, strategy, site, memory, evals, scripts, and Claude Code skills in the rest of this repository remain proprietary to **Bosser Oy**. They are not open for reuse. See the root [`COPYRIGHT.md`](../COPYRIGHT.md) for the full scope.

The split is deliberate: the **research system is a public good**; the **training product and its assets are Bosser IP**.
