**Date:** March 2026
**What's NEW (not previously captured):**

**EU/EFTA/UK tenants: Copilot Cowork is effectively blocked.** Anthropic models are disabled by default in EU, EFTA, and UK tenants. Admins must explicitly opt in. Anthropic models are excluded from the EU Data Boundary — Microsoft cannot extend standard data residency guarantees. This means:
- Nordic companies on M365 cannot use Cowork without explicit admin opt-in
- Data processed by Anthropic models flows outside EU data boundary
- Compliance teams in regulated Nordic industries (finance, healthcare) face GDPR friction
- Even after opt-in, Anthropic is a subprocessor under Microsoft's DPA — not a direct data processor relationship

**Identity issue confirmed:** Copilot Cowork executes actions using the user's identity, not a dedicated AI agent identity. No accountability model for AI-driven actions.

**Practitioner review status:** 35th consecutive cycle with zero independent hands-on reviews. We are now deep INTO the "late March" broader Frontier access window. Other Frontier features (Notebooks, Planner Agent) have shipped on schedule. Cowork has not.

**Evidence level:** Level 2-3 (multiple independent practitioner analyses of the EU/EFTA block — this is convergent signal about a structural barrier for Nordic adoption)

**Nordic implication:** This is the most significant Nordic-specific finding this cycle. For our buyer segment (large Nordic companies), Copilot Cowork requires explicit compliance review before activation. Combined with 35 cycles of zero independent reviews, this platform is not a viable near-term option for Nordic enterprises.
