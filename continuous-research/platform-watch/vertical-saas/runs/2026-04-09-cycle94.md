## Findings

### Finding 1: Agentforce Contact Center — new CCaaS platform, Level 2 evidence
**Source:** https://www.nojitter.com/ai-automation/salesforce-launches-agentforce-contact-center — [domain trade publication]
**Date:** March 10, 2026
**Agent level:** Company (CCaaS + AI agents integrated)
**What:** Native CCaaS platform combining voice, digital channels, CRM, and AI agents in one system. First time Salesforce owns its own telephony stack. Early voice containment: 40-60% (varying by industry). Named early adopters: Compass Working Capital (6,000 staff hours saved/year from post-call summarization, data entry, scheduling automation), Savant Systems (multi-audience routing + contextual data surfacing). "Contact Center 100" program — 100 early customers with intense engineering support. US/Canada only currently.
**Evidence level:** Level 2 (independent trade press, named early adopters with quantified outcomes)
**Key claims:** Voice containment 40-60% in early deployments. Compass Working Capital: 6,000 hours/year saved. Geographic limit: US/Canada only.
**Genuinely agentic?** Yes — post-call summarization, routing decisions, and data entry automation are multi-step autonomous tasks.

**Genuinely agentic?** Yes — voice agents autonomously resolving booking interactions, multi-turn, integrated with live CRM.
