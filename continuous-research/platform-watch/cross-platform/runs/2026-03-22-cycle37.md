## Findings

### Finding 1: Compound Engineering — First Named Methodology for Coding Agent Flywheel
**Source:** [https://every.to/chain-of-thought/compound-engineering-how-every-codes-with-agents](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/compound-engineering-how-every-codes-with-agents) — [practitioner direct]
**Date:** December 11, 2025 (updated March 13, 2026)
**Agent level:** Personal → Team (compounding benefits propagate)
**What:** Dan Shipper (CEO, Every) and Kieran Klaassen (engineer) coined "compound engineering" — the principle that each unit of engineering work should make subsequent units easier. The flywheel: Plan → Work → Review → Compound, where "Compound" means documenting patterns into CLAUDE.md and creating new agents. Specific claim: "a single developer can do the work of five developers a few years ago." Every runs 5 software products, each primarily built by one person, serving thousands of daily users. Developer role transforms to orchestrator — 80% time in planning/review, 20% in execution. Plugin has 5,132 GitHub stars, 426 forks, 468 commits across 51 releases (v2.48.0 as of March 22, 2026).
**Evidence level:** Level 2 (single company, specific claims, practitioner direct)
**Key claims:**
- 1 developer ≈ 5 developers output (self-reported)
- 5 products, each run by ~1 person
- 24 specialized AI agents, 13 slash commands, 11 skills, 2 MCP servers in the plugin
- Compounding mechanism: knowledge captured in CLAUDE.md compounds across sessions
- 5,132 GitHub stars signals community adoption beyond Every

## Orientation

**The coding agent flywheel (Pattern 41) now has its first named methodology and Level 2 evidence.** Compound engineering validates the core thesis: knowledge captured from each engineering cycle makes the next cycle faster. Two independent practitioners (Every + Larson/Imprint) confirm the pattern. But three gaps remain: (a) no Segment B enterprise evidence, (b) no evidence outside coding/engineering, (c) "1 developer = 5" is self-reported with no controlled comparison.

**The flywheel has a name but not yet convergence.** We need 10-20 independent practitioners reporting the same compounding pattern to upgrade from Level 2 to Level 3. The 5,132 GitHub stars and Larson's independent validation are encouraging early signals.

**Copilot Cowork silence is now the strongest finding about Microsoft's business agent position.** Six consecutive cycles with zero independent reviews. The gap between announcement ambition and deployment evidence is the widest of any current product.

**Anthropic's enterprise surface is expanding faster than any other platform** — self-serve Enterprise, private marketplaces, PwC governance layer, 11 open-source plugins. But still zero deployment outcomes data. The pattern: fastest product shipping, no deployment evidence. Same gap as everyone else, just with better products shipping into it.

**The competence gap data strengthens our positioning.** 78% of executives say AI outpaces their training. 82% have no talent strategy. This is the market we serve — the gap between tool availability and organizational readiness.
