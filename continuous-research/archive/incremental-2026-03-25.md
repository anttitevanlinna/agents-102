## EU AI Act Update (March 2026)

### Finland Enforcement — Operational, No Actions Yet
- National supervision laws in effect since Jan 1, 2026. Decentralized model: Traficom (coordinator), Data Protection Ombudsman, Tukes, Customs, Fimea, Occupational Safety.
- Sanctions Board at Traficom with fines above EUR 100K-300K. AI Sandbox rules in force since ~Feb 1, 2026.
- **No enforcement actions as of March 25, 2026.** Framework still being operationalized.
- Sources:
  - https://www.hannessnellman.com/news-and-views/blog/eu-ai-act-latest-regulatory-developments-in-finland/ [domain trade publication]
  - https://www.twobirds.com/en/capabilities/artificial-intelligence/ai-legal-services/ai-regulatory-horizon-tracker/finland [domain trade publication]
  - https://tem.fi/en/ai-regulation [government source]

### August 2, 2026 Deadline — High-Risk Systems
- Full compliance required for high-risk AI (recruitment, credit scoring, insurance pricing, etc.).
- appliedAI study: 18% of 106 enterprise AI systems clearly high-risk, 40% unclear classification.
- **Digital Omnibus Proposal:** EC proposed potential extension to Dec 2, 2027, but ONLY if harmonized standards unavailable. Not a blanket postponement.
- Penalties: up to EUR 35M or 7% global turnover.
- Source: https://ai2.work/economics/eu-ai-act-high-risk-rules-hit-august-2026-your-compliance-countdown/ [domain trade publication]

## Convergence Patterns (Updated)

### Legal AI Approaching Convergence (Level 3 Emerging)
Harvey ($11B, 100K+ lawyers) and Legora ($5.55B, 800+ firms) are both deploying multi-step autonomous agents at scale. Same pattern: agent loops with human-in-the-loop checkpoints, multi-agent pipelines, scheduled autonomous execution. This is no longer single experiments — it's two well-funded companies with thousands of customers independently reporting the same architectural shift from copilot to agent.

### Nordic Caution Gap — CONFIRMED (Level 3)
Two independent surveys confirm: Nordic companies invest in AI but are systematically more conservative on agentic AI timelines and budget allocation than European peers. BCG (54% experimenting, 60% <5% budget) + Deloitte (58% expect 3+ years ROI vs. 37% Europe). The gap between "agents arriving in your software" and "organizations not budgeting for them" is the exact competence gap Agents 102 addresses.

### Platform Announcement-to-Deployment Gap — STILL ENORMOUS
Every major enterprise platform vendor (Oracle, SAP, Microsoft, Workday, ServiceNow, Salesforce) announced agents in Q1 2026. NONE have independently verified customer deployments with named companies and measurable outcomes. No practitioner blog posts, X.com threads, or conference talks describing "I deployed Agentforce/Joule/Dynamics agents and here's what happened."
