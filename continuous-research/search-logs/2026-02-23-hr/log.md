## Notes

### Key Practitioner Signals
- **AIHR (link #12, #47):** Cites Chipotle 75% time-to-hire reduction as specific deployment result. AIHR is an independent HR analyst, not a vendor.
- **Josh Bersin (link #24):** Practitioner analyst recognizes Paradox as leading autonomous recruiting agent. However, article from April 2024 — at freshness boundary.
- **NBC News (link #28):** Independent journalism investigating AI recruiting including glitches and failures — valuable counter-evidence.
- **HR Executive (link #75):** Status report on agents moving from PoC to production — independent trade press.
- **InFeedo (link #71, #72):** Vendor but with useful failure pattern analysis: emotional complexity, rigid rules, organizational overreliance.
- **Built In (link #77):** 40%+ of AI agent projects will be canceled by 2027 (Gartner stat). Not HR-specific but relevant context.

### Deployment Evidence (Strongest)
- **Paradox/McDonald's:** Application-to-interview from 3 days to 3 minutes; 2x applications; 60% time-to-hire reduction. Multiple independent sources confirm.
- **Chipotle:** 75% time-to-hire reduction via AI recruiting agent (AIHR source).
- **Beacon Mobility/Leena AI:** 60% automated query resolution, 97.5% CSAT, 2000+ hours saved, 50.5% hiring increase YoY.
- **Medidata/Workday:** $1.46M annual savings from automated HR/finance workflows.
- **Workday ASOR:** Agent System of Record concept confirmed by independent press (diginomica, Techzine, No Jitter). Treating agents as "digital employees" with roles and cost centers.

### Counter-Evidence
- **40%+ of AI agent projects will be canceled by 2027** (Gartner via Built In).
- **Only 1% of companies have achieved AI maturity** despite 92% planning to increase AI investments (PwC).
- **Only 1 in 5 large employers has end-to-end AI orchestration** from sourcing through onboarding (AIHR).
- **NBC News reports glitches** in AI recruiting agents — independent journalism.
- **InFeedo documents failure patterns:** emotional complexity, rigid rules, teams "forget how to screen manually."

### Nordic Findings
- **Zero new Nordic-specific HR agent deployments found.** This confirms the first research round finding.
- Nordea chatbot (Boost.ai) appeared again but it's chatbot, not agentic.
- Huma and HR-Nordic offer Nordic HR tools but nothing agentic.
- Nordic AI Institute, Nordic AI Hub — none have HR agent deployments.
- **The Nordics remain consumers of global platforms (Workday, SAP, ServiceNow) for HR AI**, not builders of HR agents.
