**Source:** None found after targeted search
**What:** The "immerse → learn → return → next cohort" organizational deployment model has zero external documentation. ## Meta-learning

Tier 0 signal resolution cycles are a distinct research type. Their value is NOT in finding external convergence (often absent) but in accurately placing the signal on the evidence ladder — distinguishing "ahead of published practice" from "not real." The rotation model and skill progression findings are clearly "ahead of published practice." Analytics Vidhya's learning path and Atlassian's mob documentation were found immediately via concept-specific searches — suggesting that when the published literature IS there, even sparse, it surfaces quickly. The absence after 9 targeted searches is a reliable absence.
