## Meta-learning

Simultaneous triple-event cycles require a triage heuristic: vendor GA announcements are easy to confirm (one fetch); billing live impact requires 24–48 hour lag before independent reports surface; suspension/reinstatement tracking needs direct newsroom fetches plus secondary crowd signals (prediction markets proved highly useful here). The Fable 5 suspension created a secondary information-suppression effect — Willison's silence during the suspension period is itself a signal, as he was the most active primary source in the pre-suspension period. Future suspension tracking should explicitly monitor prediction market volume and odds as a real-time sentiment proxy when primary-source updates are absent.
